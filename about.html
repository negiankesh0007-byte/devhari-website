Who We Are

Devhari Foundation is a registered non-profit society dedicated to serving the community with dignity, compassion, and equality. We focus on education, health, and welfare to uplift underserved populations in Vadodara, Gujarat.

Vision

To create a society where every individual has access to basic rights and opportunities for a better life.

Mission

Empowering communities through targeted programs in education, health, and welfare, fostering sustainable change.
